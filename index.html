Espresso

A strong and concentrated coffee made by forcing hot water through finely-ground coffee beans. It forms the base for many other coffee drinks and is known for its bold flavor and thick crema on top.

Cappuccino

A coffee drink known for its creamy texture and smooth, balanced flavor. This coffee beverage is made with equal parts espresso, steamed milk, and milk foam on top.

Latte

Latte Made with espresso and steamed milk, a latte is milkier than a cappuccino and has a smooth, mild taste. It's often served with a thin layer of foam and can be flavored with syrups like vanilla or caramel.

Affogato

Affogato A dessert-style coffee made by pouring a shot of hot espresso over a scoop of vanilla ice cream, offering a delicious blend of hot and cold, bitter and sweet.

The Coffee Gazette

"The Coffee Gazette" could be the name of a blog, newsletter, magazine, or other media outlet that delivers news, stories, and insights about coffee, acting as a guide or announcer for coffee enthusiasts.
